How to Check the Age of a LinkedIn Account

1

Via LinkedIn Mobile App

• Open the LinkedIn application on your smartphone

• Click on your profile photo

• Click the 3 (three) dots at the bottom of the profile photo

• Select "About Profile"

• View account creation date

Figure 1: About Profile Menu in the LinkedIn App
2

Via Desktop Browser

• Open LinkedIn.com in a desktop browser

• Click on your profile picture in the top right corner

• Select "View Profile" from the dropdown menu

• Scroll to the bottom of the profile page

• Click the 3 dots on the side of the profile then click About Profile

Service Operational Details

In operating LinkedIn account rental services, there are several things you need to know regarding the possibility of restricted accounts:

Possibility of Restricted Account

IMPORTANT: In some cases, LinkedIn accounts may experience restricted access or temporary restrictions. This is normal and can be resolved.

Access from Different Devices

LinkedIn may restrict accounts being accessed from two different devices simultaneously

Sudden Profile Change

Significant changes to profile information may trigger LinkedIn's security systems.

Solution for Restricted Account

1

Identity Verification

• LinkedIn will ask for identity verification via email or SMS

• The verification process usually takes 24-48 hours

• Make sure your account contact information is valid and active.

2

Special Group Support

• We provide a special WhatsApp group to help with restricted account issues.

• The support team will provide step by step guidance.

• Collective experience helps resolve issues faster

REST ASAP: Restricted accounts are a common occurrence in LinkedIn marketing operations. Over 80% of cases can be resolved within 48 hours with proper identity verification.
